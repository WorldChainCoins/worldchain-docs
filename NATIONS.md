# World Chain Coins Nations
 
World Chain Coins connects individual nation coins, communities, cultures, identities and digital worlds through the main ecosystem token, **$WORLDCHAIN**.

Each addition to the ecosystem has its own character, visual identity, story, community and place inside one continuously expanding global collection.

The following nation coins are currently part of the live World Chain Coins ecosystem:

## Nation Identity

Every nation can include:

- Its own Solana token
- A unique Country Kid
- A dedicated visual identity
- An individual community
- A detailed nation page
- Cultural and historical information
- Traditions, food and music
- Landmarks and nature
- Fun facts
- Crypto identity
- Official coin information
- A place inside the global collection

## Community-Unlocked Nations

The World Chain Coins community can help decide what joins the ecosystem next.

Verified $WORLDCHAIN holders can participate in voting and contribute toward unlock milestones.

Turkey became the first nation unlocked through community voting.

China became the second nation unlocked by the community.

Future voting rounds may unlock countries, territories, stateless nations, cultural identities, communities, special concepts or entirely new parts of the ecosystem.

## Beyond Traditional Nations

World Chain Coins is designed as an open ecosystem.

It is not limited to geography, current borders or existing political classifications.

A future addition can represent a nation, people, culture, territory, community, identity, idea, world or collective.

Each addition can develop its own:

- Token
- Character
- Visual style
- Story
- Community
- Collection
- Interactive features
- Events
- Quizzes
- Games
- Digital collectibles
- Future utility

The structure remains open so that World Chain Coins can continue growing without being forced into one fixed model.

## Collections

Nations and all future ecosystem additions can become part of the World Chain Coins collection system.

Collectors may be able to:

- Discover which ecosystem assets they own
- Track missing additions
- Complete regional, cultural, thematic or global collections
- Unlock collector ranks
- Earn achievements
- Display collection cards
- Collect special editions
- Participate in future collection challenges
- Build a personal World Chain
- Share their progress with the community

Future collections may also include NFTs, digital badges, rare Country Kids, event items, community rewards and other collectible assets.

The collection system is intended to evolve together with the wider ecosystem.
