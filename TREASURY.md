# World Chain Coins Treasury

The World Chain Coins treasury section is designed to make important ecosystem wallet activity publicly visible and easier to verify.

World Chain Coins uses public Solana blockchain data to display information connected to ecosystem wallets.

## Treasury Purpose

The treasury may support different areas of the World Chain Coins ecosystem, including:

- Ecosystem development
- Technical infrastructure
- Platform expansion
- Community growth
- Marketing
- Content creation
- Nation launches
- Partnerships
- Future features
- Unexpected ecosystem opportunities

The exact use of treasury resources may evolve as World Chain Coins continues to grow.

## Public Wallet Information

The official platform can display public information such as:

- Treasury wallet balances
- Marketing wallet balances
- SOL holdings
- Transaction history
- Wallet activity
- Public blockchain movements
- Links to trusted Solana explorers

All displayed information is based on publicly available blockchain data.

## Blockchain Verification

Users should independently verify treasury information through trusted Solana blockchain explorers.

Public blockchain data can include:

- Incoming transactions
- Outgoing transactions
- Wallet balances
- Token transfers
- Transaction signatures
- Timestamps
- Network fees

Blockchain explorers remain the primary source for independently checking on-chain activity.

## Treasury Wallets

Official treasury and ecosystem wallet addresses are only valid when published through verified World Chain Coins channels.

Users should always compare wallet addresses with:

- The official website
- `OFFICIAL-LINKS.md`
- Official World Chain Coins social accounts
- Official Telegram channels
- Trusted Solana explorers

Never trust wallet addresses received through unsolicited private messages.

## Marketing Wallet

World Chain Coins may use separate public wallets for marketing, growth, development or other ecosystem purposes.

These wallets may be displayed independently to make their activity easier to follow.

The existence of a public wallet does not mean that every transaction can immediately be assigned to a specific public activity.

Additional explanations may be published through official updates when appropriate.

## Security

World Chain Coins will never ask users to send funds to a treasury wallet through an unsolicited private message.

Always verify:

- The official wallet address
- The destination network
- The transaction amount
- The source of the request
- The official announcement
- The blockchain explorer link

Blockchain transactions are normally irreversible.
