# World Chain Coins Telegram Ecosystem

The World Chain Coins Telegram ecosystem connects project information, nation coins, community updates and automated blockchain activity in one accessible communication environment.

Telegram is used to help users navigate the ecosystem, verify information and follow important developments.

## Telegram Bot

The World Chain Coins Telegram bot can provide information about:

- The main $WORLDCHAIN ecosystem token
- Live nation coins
- Official contract addresses
- Nation pages
- Trading links
- Community features
- Ecosystem navigation
- Public updates
- Future platform functions

The bot may continue expanding as new nations, tools and ecosystem areas are introduced.

## Buy Notifications

World Chain Coins includes automated buy notifications for supported ecosystem tokens.

The buybot can use blockchain data to identify genuine purchases and publish information such as:

- The purchased nation coin
- The amount purchased
- The buyer wallet
- The transaction value
- A blockchain explorer link
- A direct trading link
- A connection to the wider World Chain Coins ecosystem

The system is designed to distinguish purchases from unrelated transfers or other blockchain activity whenever possible.

## Nation Integration

New live nation coins can be connected to the Telegram ecosystem.

Once officially integrated, a nation may become available through:

- Bot commands
- Inline navigation
- Contract verification
- Trading links
- Buy notifications
- Ecosystem updates
- Community announcements

The structure is designed to support continuous expansion.

## Official Channels

Only Telegram groups, bots and channels listed through official World Chain Coins sources should be trusted.

Users should verify Telegram links through:

- The official World Chain Coins website
- `OFFICIAL-LINKS.md`
- The official GitHub organization
- Verified World Chain Coins social accounts

A Telegram username, logo or display name alone does not prove that an account is official.

## Telegram Safety

World Chain Coins representatives will never ask users through private messages for:

- Seed phrases
- Private keys
- Wallet recovery phrases
- Passwords
- Remote access
- Unsolicited token transfers
- Direct payments to unknown wallets

Users should be cautious of:

- Fake support accounts
- Impersonated administrators
- Fake giveaways
- Fraudulent token links
- False contract addresses
- Wallet-draining websites
- Unsolicited private messages

Always verify information before interacting with a wallet or transaction.
