# World Chain Coins Community Voting

Community voting gives verified $WORLDCHAIN holders an active role in the expansion of the World Chain Coins ecosystem.

The voting system is designed to help the community influence which nations, identities, concepts, worlds or future additions become part of the ecosystem next.

## How Voting Works

Eligible users can connect a compatible Solana wallet and participate in active voting rounds.

The platform can verify:

- Wallet connection
- $WORLDCHAIN holdings
- Voting eligibility
- Voting power
- Previous participation
- Active candidates
- Current voting progress
- Unlock milestones

Voting rules may vary between different rounds and future ecosystem features.

## Voting Power

Voting power can be connected to verified $WORLDCHAIN holdings.

The system may use holder-based weighting, participation limits or other mechanisms depending on the purpose of a voting round.

The exact structure can evolve as World Chain Coins grows.

## What the Community Can Vote For

Community voting is not limited to one fixed category.

Future voting rounds may include:

- New nations
- Territories
- Communities
- Cultural identities
- Special concepts
- Digital worlds
- Collection themes
- Platform features
- Community events
- Interactive experiences
- Future ideas proposed by the ecosystem

World Chain Coins remains open to new forms of participation and expansion.

## Community-Unlocked Nations

Turkey became the first nation unlocked through World Chain Coins community voting.

China became the second nation unlocked by the community.

These milestones demonstrated that holders can directly contribute to the growth of the ecosystem.

## Unlock Milestones

Voting rounds may use a defined unlock target.

When a candidate reaches the required milestone, it can move into the next stage of development or launch preparation.

Reaching an unlock target does not always guarantee an immediate launch.

Preparation may still include:

- Visual development
- Country Kid creation
- Token setup
- Nation page content
- Technical integration
- Community preparation
- Security checks
- Launch planning

## Future Voting Features

The voting system may continue expanding through:

- New voting categories
- Additional holder verification
- Different voting-power models
- Community proposals
- Ranked voting
- Time-limited voting rounds
- Special event voting
- Feature prioritization
- Collection decisions
- Community governance tools
- New participation mechanics

No fixed structure permanently limits how voting may evolve.

## Fair Participation

World Chain Coins aims to make voting transparent and understandable.

Voting systems may include protection against:

- Duplicate participation
- Invalid wallets
- Manipulated submissions
- Ineligible votes
- Technical abuse
- Automated spam
- Misleading results

Blockchain data and server-side verification may be used to support fair participation.

## Official Results

Voting results and unlock announcements are only official when published through verified World Chain Coins channels.

Users should always confirm results through:

- The official website
- Official social channels
- Official Telegram channels
- The official GitHub organization
- Public ecosystem updates

## Open Development

Community voting is a long-term part of World Chain Coins.

The system can continue evolving as new technologies, community ideas and ecosystem features are introduced.

Future voting may influence much more than individual nation launches.
