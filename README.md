# World Chain Coins

**One World. One Chain. Connected Together.**

World Chain Coins is a growing Solana ecosystem that connects individual nation coins through one central ecosystem token: **$WORLDCHAIN**.

Every nation has its own coin, Country Kid, visual identity, community and story. Instead of existing as isolated tokens, all nations belong to one connected and continuously expanding digital world.

The goal is to build a global collectible ecosystem in which countries, cultures and communities come together through blockchain technology.

## How the Ecosystem Works

World Chain Coins combines a main ecosystem token with a growing collection of individual nation coins.

Every nation receives:

- Its own Solana token
- A unique Country Kid
- A recognizable visual identity
- Its own community
- A dedicated nation page
- Cultural and educational content
- A place inside the global collection

Each new nation expands the connected world and adds another collectible part to the ecosystem.

$WORLDCHAIN remains the common link between all nations and future platform developments.

## Wallet-Based Collection System

World Chain Coins includes a wallet-based collection experience.

Users can connect a compatible Solana wallet and discover:

- Which nation coins they own
- Which nations are still missing
- Their current collection progress
- Their collector rank
- Their active World Chain
- Their progress toward higher collector tiers

The collection system transforms individual nation coins into one connected global collection.

Collection cards can also be visually displayed and exported, allowing holders to share their progress with the community.

## Community Voting

The World Chain Coins community helps shape the future of the ecosystem.

Verified $WORLDCHAIN holders can connect their wallet and vote for upcoming nations.

Votes accumulate toward a defined unlock goal. When a nation reaches the required number of votes, it is unlocked by the community and moves into launch preparation.

This gives holders an active role in deciding how the connected world continues to grow.

Turkey became the first nation unlocked through community voting, followed by China.

## Telegram Ecosystem

World Chain Coins uses dedicated Telegram tools to connect the website, nation coins and community.

The Telegram system can provide:

- Official ecosystem information
- Nation coin details
- Verified contract addresses
- Direct trading links
- Website navigation
- Community updates
- Automated buy notifications

The World Chain Coins buybot monitors live nation coins and can recognize genuine purchases through blockchain data.

When a country coin is purchased, the notification connects that purchase back to the wider World Chain Coins ecosystem.

## Built to Expand
