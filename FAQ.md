# World Chain Coins FAQ

## What is World Chain Coins?

World Chain Coins is a growing Solana ecosystem that connects individual nation coins, communities, digital identities, collectibles and future platform features through the main ecosystem token, **$WORLDCHAIN**.

## What is $WORLDCHAIN?

$WORLDCHAIN is the central ecosystem token.

It connects the current and future parts of World Chain Coins, including nation coins, collections, community voting, collector progression, platform features and future expansions.

## Is World Chain Coins limited to countries?

No.

World Chain Coins can expand beyond officially recognized countries and may include communities, cultures, identities, territories, special concepts, themed worlds, digital collections and entirely new ideas.

The ecosystem is intentionally open and not limited to one fixed category.

## What is a Nation Coin?

A Nation Coin is an individual Solana token representing a nation or another ecosystem identity.

Each Nation Coin can have its own:

- Country Kid
- Visual identity
- Community
- Story
- Nation page
- Cultural content
- Contract address
- Place inside the World Chain Coins collection

## What are Country Kids?

Country Kids are the recognizable characters representing individual nations and ecosystem identities.

They create a consistent visual connection between all parts of World Chain Coins while giving every nation its own personality.

## How does the collection system work?

Users can connect a compatible Solana wallet and view which World Chain Coins assets they hold.

The platform can show:

- Collected nations
- Missing nations
- Collection progress
- Collector ranks
- Progression levels
- Shareable collection cards
- Future achievements and rewards

## How does community voting work?

Verified $WORLDCHAIN holders can participate in community voting.

The community can help decide which nation, identity, concept or future ecosystem addition should be unlocked next.

## Which nations were unlocked by the community?

Turkey became the first nation unlocked through community voting.

China became the second nation unlocked by the community.

## Will there be more nations?

Yes.

World Chain Coins is designed for continuous expansion.

There is no fixed final number of nations, communities, identities, collections or future worlds.
