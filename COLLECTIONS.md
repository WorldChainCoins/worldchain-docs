# World Chain Coins Collections

The World Chain Coins collection system connects individual nation coins, future ecosystem assets and digital identities into one continuously growing collection.

Instead of viewing every token as an isolated asset, collectors can build their own personal World Chain across the entire ecosystem.

## Wallet-Based Collection

Users can connect a compatible Solana wallet to discover which World Chain Coins assets they hold.

The platform can identify:

- Collected nation coins
- Missing nation coins
- Personal collection progress
- Collector status
- Progression toward higher collector levels
- Future special assets and digital collectibles

Wallet ownership is verified through public blockchain data.

## Personal World Chain

Every collector can build an individual World Chain based on the ecosystem assets held inside their wallet.

A personal World Chain may include:

- Nation coins
- Community-unlocked nations
- Special editions
- Future digital collectibles
- Event-based assets
- Achievement items
- NFTs
- Future ecosystem additions

The system is designed to remain expandable as new types of assets are introduced.

## Collector Progression

Collectors can progress through different levels based on their participation and collection.

Future progression systems may consider:

- Number of collected nations
- Completion of specific groups
- Community participation
- Special achievements
- Rare ecosystem assets
- Event participation
- Long-term collector activity
- Future ecosystem mechanics

Collector ranks and progression systems may continue evolving as World Chain Coins grows.

## Collection Cards

World Chain Coins can display personal collection cards that allow users to present their progress visually.

Collection cards may include:

- Wallet identity
- Collected nations
- Missing nations
- Collection percentage
- Collector rank
- Achievements
- Special assets
- Shareable visual summaries

These cards can help collectors share their World Chain with the wider community.
