# World Chain Coins Security

World Chain Coins is committed to protecting its community through clear verification, transparent communication and responsible security practices.

## Official Verification

Always verify information through the official World Chain Coins channels.

Before connecting a wallet, trading or sending funds, check:

- The official website
- The official GitHub organization
- The official social media accounts
- The official contract addresses
- The official nation coin links
- Trusted Solana blockchain explorers

Official links are listed in:

`OFFICIAL-LINKS.md`

## Wallet Safety

World Chain Coins will never ask for:

- Your seed phrase
- Your private key
- Your wallet recovery phrase
- Remote access to your device
- Your password
- A direct payment through an unsolicited private message

Never share private wallet information with anyone.

## Fake Tokens and Impersonation

Scammers may create fake tokens, websites, social media accounts or Telegram profiles using the World Chain Coins name.

Always verify:

- Token contract addresses
- Website domains
- Social media usernames
- Telegram channels
- Wallet addresses
- Trading links

A token name or logo alone does not prove that a project is official.

## Smart Contract and Trading Risk

World Chain Coins and its associated nation coins are crypto assets and involve financial risk.

Blockchain transactions are normally irreversible.

Users are responsible for checking all transaction details before confirming them.

Nothing in this repository constitutes financial, legal, tax or investment advice.

## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security problem, suspicious account, fake token or impersonation attempt, report it through an official World Chain Coins communication channel.

Do not publish sensitive technical details publicly before the issue has been reviewed.

## Public Repository Scope

This repository contains public documentation only.

The complete website source code, internal APIs, automation systems, private infrastructure, credentials and security-sensitive configuration are not published here.

## Important Reminder

World Chain Coins representatives will never contact you privately to request wallet access or confidential information.

Stay cautious, verify everything and never rush a blockchain transaction.
