# World Chain Coins Ecosystem

World Chain Coins is a continuously expanding Solana ecosystem that connects nations, communities, identities, collections, digital experiences and future concepts through the main ecosystem token, **$WORLDCHAIN**.

The ecosystem is designed as one connected structure rather than a collection of unrelated tokens.

Every new addition can have its own identity while remaining connected to the wider World Chain Coins universe.

## The Central Connection

At the center of the ecosystem is **$WORLDCHAIN**.

$WORLDCHAIN connects current and future areas such as:

- Nation coins
- Community-unlocked additions
- Country Kids
- Collections
- Collector progression
- Community voting
- Referrals
- Leaderboards
- Treasury transparency
- Telegram tools
- Multilingual content
- Interactive features
- Digital collectibles
- Future platform expansions

The exact role of $WORLDCHAIN can continue evolving as new parts of the ecosystem are developed.
