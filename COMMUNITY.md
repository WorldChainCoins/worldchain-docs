# World Chain Coins Community

The World Chain Coins community is an active part of the ecosystem.

World Chain Coins is designed to grow through participation, collecting, voting, sharing, creating and connecting communities around the world.

The community is not limited to one country, language, platform or type of participant.

## Community Participation

Community members can support the ecosystem through:

- Holding $WORLDCHAIN
- Collecting nation coins
- Participating in community voting
- Sharing World Chain Coins content
- Inviting new community members
- Taking part in discussions
- Joining events and challenges
- Supporting individual nation communities
- Providing ideas and feedback
- Helping the ecosystem grow internationally

Participation may expand into additional formats as new platform features are introduced.

## Community Voting

Verified $WORLDCHAIN holders can help influence future ecosystem expansion.

Community voting may be used to decide:

- Which nation joins next
- Which concept should be developed
- Which special addition should be unlocked
- Which community milestone should be pursued
- Which future feature receives priority

Voting structures may evolve as World Chain Coins develops.

## Referrals and Growth

World Chain Coins includes community growth and referral features.

Community members may be able to:

- Create personal referral links
- Invite new participants
- Track verified referrals
- Build a visible community position
- Appear on leaderboards
- Contribute to global growth milestones
- Unlock future achievements or recognition

The purpose of referrals is to make community growth visible and reward meaningful participation.

## Nation Communities

Every nation can develop its own identity and community while remaining connected to the wider World Chain Coins ecosystem.

Nation communities may create their own:

- Discussions
- Events
- Content
- Traditions
- Challenges
- Memes
- Campaigns
- Collector groups
- Cultural contributions
- Community stories

This allows individual communities to grow without becoming disconnected from the main ecosystem.

## Community Grid

World Chain Coins may introduce a permanent digital community grid.

The grid could allow supporters to leave a visible and lasting mark inside the ecosystem.

A community space may contain:

- A name
- A username
- A message
- A wallet identity
- A nation
- An image
- A symbol
- A link
- An achievement
- A personal contribution

The final form of the community grid is not fixed and may develop into a larger interactive experience.

## Community Safety

Community members should always remain cautious.

World Chain Coins will never ask for:

- Seed phrases
- Private keys
- Wallet recovery phrases
- Passwords
- Remote access
- Unsolicited payments through private messages

Always verify accounts, links, contracts and announcements through official World Chain Coins channels.
